Visual Designer

is a passion project for plant lovers.

March '21 - Present

____________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Role: I started this project with three other friends with the goal of producing an app for plant lovers. With two friends initiating the idea, our team saw a lack of plant apps that allow you to track propagations. This is where Propagate comes in. Beyond the basics of a plant app, Propagate will also grow your community through the propagations you share to your friends.
Note: This project is still in the making. As it is a passion project, there's a never ending MVP list we're working on. Below are previews of what the branding and visuals will be like. Reach out to me if you'd like to hear more about the project!
